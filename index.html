Happy Birthday Nana!

Hi my beautiful princess, hopefully, you slept well last night! I had little time to prepare for this, but I still did it to show you how I feel. Nana, happy birthday my love! I know that I always tell you this, but I really do love you. You are the best thing that could have happened. From the very start of our conversation, you were and still are a wonderful angel that fell straight from heaven. It is unreal to think that I had the opportunity to meet such a person; sometimes, I think that I am dreaming, a dream that I never want to wake up from.

I cannot believe that a cute beautiful angel brightened my gloomy life. You are the first and last star that formed in my universe, a star that shines so bright, it illuminates all unreachable areas in my universe. Like the moon, revolve around you, you are my world. You are everything!

Wherever I think about the future, a picture of something appears in my mind, a picture that gets progressively brighter, a future where you and I live happily together. If I ever fall in the deepest pity of sorrow, I know that your mere existence will immediately lift me up from there.

Just the two of us, I wish to show many things and grant all of your wishes. I want you to be the happiest woman, no, the happiest person in the world. I do not guarantee that our road ahead will not change, it might even be bumpy at times, but something that will never change will be my love for you. I love you baby, I love every part of you, even those that you do not like.

Despite death being a threat to us, I want to spend all my remaining life with you. I want to be with you and I want to see you achieve all of your dreams. And if you cannot do it alone, I will always be here to help you regardless of our distance. I will be with you even if you get sick of me, I will always be with you like an attached puppy.

May 3rd marks the day in which you were born, a date that I will never forget. It is another year in your life, so baby…Congratulations!!! I love you my fiancée and one day, we will celebrate your birthday together in person.

I love you Nana!
